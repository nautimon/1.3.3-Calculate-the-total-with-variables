Again, this one will look like one of yesterday's assignments, but using variables! 

Create a variable called `bill` and assign it to a subtotal of 32.15.

Next, create a variable called `tax` and assign it the result of multiplying `bill` by an 8% tax rate. (Hint: 8% in decimal form is written as 0.08.)

Then, create a variable called `tip` and assign it to the value of the tip, which should be 20% of the bill (Hint: this can be found by multiplying the `bill` by 0.2).

Finally, add the `bill`, `tax`, and `tip` together and store it into a variable called `total`. Then, round your total to two decimal points and print it to the console.
